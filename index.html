Select from the list then click anywhere on the map.

Markers can be dragged. Double click a marker to remove.

When a marker is added, moved, or deleted, markers will be broadcasted to other clients.

When the page is loaded, there won't be any markers until an event is broadcasted.

If you want to collaborate, and your teammates have already placed markers, wait until they broadcast an event or you may overwrite some of their markers.
